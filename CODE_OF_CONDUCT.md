# Code of Conduct

MindLeap AI Labs is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free project community.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive and considerate.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Respect privacy and never request real conversation exports.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Help keep discussions focused and useful.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing another person's private information
- Sharing sensitive exports or credentials
- Deliberate disruption or spam

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate this policy and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainers.
